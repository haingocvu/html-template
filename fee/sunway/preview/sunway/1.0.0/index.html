About Us

The place you won't want to leave

The Sunway hotel is located in one block from a subway station and within walking distance of Central Park. It features a restaurant, meeting and banquet facilisities and rooms with a flat-screen TV.

Guests have access to two balconies which offer wonderful views of the city. We also speak your language!
